Our Story

Founded by two brothers from Kathmandu, Himalayan Kitchen was born out of a deep homesickness for the bustling streets of Thamel and the aroma of street-side Momos.

Our mission is simple: to bring the authentic, unfiltered taste of Nepal to Sydney. Every spice is carefully sourced, our dough is hand-kneaded daily, and our curries are slow-cooked to perfection. When you dine with us, you're eating with family.
